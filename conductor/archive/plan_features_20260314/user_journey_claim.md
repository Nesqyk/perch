# User Journey: Find and Claim a Spot

**User**: Paolo (Low-Batt Procrastinator)
**Goal**: Find a spot with an outlet to finish his project.

## Journey Steps

1.  **Entry (Trigger)**: Paolo's laptop hits 15%. He opens Perch on his phone.
2.  **Discovery (The Map)**: Perch loads immediately showing the campus map with colored pins.
3.  **Filtering (Needs Matching)**:
    - Paolo taps the **"Filter"** button.
    - He selects **"Power Outlet Required"**.
    - He sets group size to **"Solo"**.
    - He taps **"Apply"**.
4.  **Selection (Choosing a Spot)**:
    - The map dims non-matching spots.
    - Paolo sees a **Green Pin** (Free) at the Library 3rd Floor.
    - He taps the pin to see the **Spot Card**.
    - The card shows: "🔇 Quiet | ⚡ Outlets | 👤 8/20 capacity | 95% Confidence".
5.  **Commitment (Claiming)**:
    - Paolo taps **"Claim Spot"**.
    - The app confirms: "Spot claimed for 30 minutes! Your timer has started."
    - The map pin turns **Blue** (Claimed) for everyone else.
6.  **Navigation (The Walk)**: Paolo walks to the library using the map as a guide.
7.  **Arrival (Success)**: Paolo arrives, finds the seat, and plugs in.
8.  **Passive Feedback (Maintenance)**: 15 minutes later, Perch sends a push notification (if enabled) or a web prompt: "Is it still quiet here?" Paolo taps "Yes."
