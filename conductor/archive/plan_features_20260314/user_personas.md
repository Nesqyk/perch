# User Personas: Filipino College Students

## 1. The "Mobile Warrior" (The Data-Strapped Commuter)
- **Name**: Jun
- **Context**: 3rd-year engineering student. Commutes 1.5 hours one-way.
- **Problem**: His professor moved the 1:00 PM class to Zoom at 12:30 PM. He is already on campus and cannot go home.
- **Needs**: High-speed Wi-Fi (he's low on mobile data) and a quiet spot to hear the lecture.
- **Tech**: Entry-level Android phone, relies on university Wi-Fi.

## 2.  **The "SQUAD Leader" (The Coordinator)**
- **Name**: Bea
- **Context**: 2nd-year Accountancy student. Leader of a 5-person study group.
- **Problem**: Needs to find a spot that can accommodate her whole group for a collaborative project after their face-to-face class was dismissed early.
- **Needs**: Large capacity spot (table for 5+), outlets for laptops, and a "moderate" noise baseline (so they can talk without being shushed).
- **Tech**: iPhone, active on Messenger and Discord.

## 3.  **The "Low-Batt Procrastinator" (The Power-Seeker)**
- **Name**: Paolo
- **Context**: Senior Fine Arts student.
- **Problem**: Needs to finish a digital illustration. His laptop is at 15%. The library outlets are all taken.
- **Needs**: **MUST** have an outlet. Proximity to food is a plus because he hasn't eaten lunch.
- **Tech**: MacBook Pro, high power consumption.

## 4.  **The "Introverted Scout" (The Quiet-Seeker)**
- **Name**: Mika
- **Context**: 1st-year Psychology student.
- **Problem**: Overwhelmed by the noise of the student center. Needs a secluded spot to read.
- **Needs**: Very quiet environment, off-campus is okay if it's within 10 mins walk.
- **Tech**: iPad and physical books.
