# MVP Feature Set Definition

The Minimum Viable Product (MVP) for Perch focuses on the core problem: "I need to find a spot right now."

## Core MVP Features (Must Have)
1.  **Campus Map Visualization**: Interactive map with all study spots plotted.
2.  **Real-time Availability Status**: Immediate visual indication of whether a spot is Free, Maybe, or Full.
3.  **Basic Filtering**: Filter spots by "Outlet availability" and "Group Size" (Rough Capacity).
4.  **Anonymous Claiming**: Allow a student to "claim" a spot for 30 minutes to signal they are heading there.
5.  **Manual Reporting**: Allow users to report "This spot is full" or "This spot is empty" to update the map for everyone.
6.  **Confidence Badge**: A simple score showing how recent/reliable the spot status is.

## Post-MVP High Priority (P1)
- **Group Coordination Beacons**: Private group layers for squad-specific pins.
- **Vibe Confirm**: Passive prompts for active users.
- **Power Priority Mode**: Dedicated outlet-first interface.

## Success Metric for MVP
- **Reduced Roaming Time**: Average time from opening the app to arriving at a spot is less than 5 minutes.
- **Data Freshness**: >70% of spots have been updated or claimed within the last 60 minutes during peak campus hours.
