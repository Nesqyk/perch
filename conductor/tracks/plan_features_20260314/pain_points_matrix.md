# Pain Points and Needs Matrix

| Persona | Primary Pain Point | Core Need | Key Feature Impact |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Mobile Warrior (Jun)** | Inability to attend online class due to sudden room change and low data. | A reliable, quiet spot with high-speed Wi-Fi. | "Wi-Fi Strength" filter and "Noise Baseline" indicator. |
| **SQUAD Leader (Bea)** | Time wasted roaming with a large group only to find spots are full. | Real-time visibility of spot capacity for groups. | "Group Size" filter and real-time "Occupied" status updates. |
| **Low-Batt Procrastinator (Paolo)** | Anxiety over device shutdown while working on a deadline. | Guaranteed access to a power outlet. | "Has Outlets" filter and visual icon on map markers. |
| **Introverted Scout (Mika)** | Sensory overload in common areas; difficulty finding secluded spots. | Discovery of low-traffic, quiet locations. | "Noise Baseline: Quiet" filter and "Off-Campus" spot inclusion. |

## Universal Needs (All Personas)
1.  **Trustworthy Data**: All users need to know if the information on the map is current (hence the "Confidence Score").
2.  **Zero-Friction Access**: Students are in a rush; the app must load quickly and require no login to see the map.
3.  **Proximity Awareness**: Knowing exactly how many minutes it takes to walk to a spot from their current location.
