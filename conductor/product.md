# Product Definition

## Vision
Perch is a real-time, map-based web app that helps Filipino college students find available study spots when class shifts online. 

## Target Audience
- **College Students**: Students needing immediate study spots when classes shift online.
- **Remote Workers**: People looking for a quiet place to work or study remotely.
- **Academic Groups**: Students coordinating with their peers for group study sessions.

## Core Goals
- **Instant Access**: Real-time map showing available study spots.
- **Crowdsourced Data**: Allowing users to claim a spot and share it with others.
- **Smart Recommendations**: Ranking spots based on distance, capacity, and confidence.

## Key Features
- **Find My Spot**: Intelligent ranking of top 3 study locations with walk-time estimates and integrated map navigation.
- **Campus Map Visualization**: Interactive map with all study spots plotted.
- **Real-time Availability Status**: Immediate visual indication of whether a spot is Free, Maybe, or Full.
- **Basic Filtering**: Filter spots by "Outlet availability" and "Group Size" (Rough Capacity).
- **Anonymous Claiming**: Allow a student to "claim" a spot for 30 minutes.
- **Manual Reporting**: User-driven status updates to improve data accuracy.
- **Confidence Badge**: A simple score showing how reliable/recent the data is.

## Platform Focus
- **Responsive Design**: Seamless experience across all devices.
