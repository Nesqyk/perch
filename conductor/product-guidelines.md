# Product Guidelines

## Brand & Voice
- **Voice**: Friendly, student-focused, and efficient.
- **Style**: Direct and informative. Avoid corporate jargon.
- **Branding**: Clean, minimal design that emphasizes map interaction.

## UI/UX Principles
- **Clarity**: Status changes (claims, reports) must be visually clear and immediate.
- **Simplicity**: No account needed; minimize the steps between opening the app and finding a spot.
- **Map-Centric**: The map is the primary navigation; keep controls within easy reach.
- **Responsive**: Mobile-first approach, ensuring map interactions are smooth on touchscreens.

## Communication
- **Errors**: Friendly and helpful error messages with actionable steps.
- **Success**: Clear visual confirmation for claims and reports.
